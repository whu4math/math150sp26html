4.4 Laws of Logarithms

Basic Properties from 4.3

  • \(a^y=x \iff \log_a x =y\)

  • \(\log_a a^x=x\), \(\ln e^x=x\)

  • \(a^{\log a^x}=x\), \(e^{\ln x}=x\)

  • \(\log_a a=1\), \(\ln e=1\)

  • \(\log_a 1=0\), \(\ln 1=0\)

Laws of Logarithms

  • \(\log_a(AB) = \log_a A + \log_a B\)

    Proof.

    Let \(\log_a A = m\) and \(\log_a B = n\). Then \(a^m = A\) and \(a^n = B\).

    \(\log_a(AB) = \log_a(a^m a^n) = \log_a a^{m+n} = m+n= \log_a A + \log_a B\)

  • \(\log_a\left(\frac{A}{B}\right) = \log_a A - \log_a B\)

    Proof.

    \(\log_a A=\log_a \left(\dfrac{A}{B}\cdot B\right)= \log_a\left(\frac{A}{B}\right)+ \log_a B\)

    \(\Rightarrow \log_a\left(\frac{A}{B}\right) = \log_a A - \log_a B\)

  • \(\log_a A^C = C\log_a A\)

    Proof.

    Let \(\log_a A = m\) Then \(a^m = A\)

    \(\log_a A^C = \log_a (a^m)^C= \log_a a^{mC} = mC = C\log_a A\)

Remark: These laws go both ways. We can expand a log function into a sum of simpler log functions or combine a sum of log functions into a single log function.

  1. Evaluate.

    1. \(\log_2 60 - \log_2 15= \log_2 \frac{60}{15}= \log_2 4=2\)

    2. \(\log 50 + \log 200= \log(50 \cdot 200)= \log 10000=4\) because \(10^4 = 10000\)

    3. \(\frac{1}{4}\log_3 81= \frac{1}{4}\log_3 3^4= 4 \cdot \frac{1}{4} \cdot \log_3 3=1\)

  2. Expand.

    1. \(\log_3(8x)\) \[\begin{align*} &= \log_3 8 + \log_3 x \\ &= \log_3 2^3 + \log_3 x \\ &= 3\log_3 2 + \log_3 x \end{align*}\]

    2. \(\log_2(AB^2)\) \[\begin{align*} &= \log_2 A + \log_2 B^2 \\ &= \log_2 A + 2\log_2 B \end{align*}\]

    3. \(\log_3\left(\frac{\sqrt{3x^5}}{y}\right)\) \[\begin{align*} &= \log_3(3x^5)^{1/2} - \log_3 y\\ &= \frac{1}{2}\log_3(3x^5) - \log_3 y \\ &= \frac{1}{2}(\log_3 3 + \log_3 x^5) - \log_3 y \\ &= \frac{1}{2} + \frac{5}{2}\log_3 x - \log_3 y \end{align*}\]

  3. Combine.

    1. \(2\log x - 3\log(x+1)\)

      move the power back and then combine. \[\begin{align*} &= \log x^2 - \log(x+1)^3\\ &= \log \frac{x^2}{(x+1)^3} \end{align*}\]

    2. \(4\log x - \frac{1}{3}\log(x^2+1) + 2\log(x-1)\)

      \[\begin{align*} &= \log x^4 - \log(x^2+1)^{1/3} + \log(x-1)^2 \\ &= \log \frac{x^4}{(x^2+1)^{1/3}} + \log(x-1)^2 \\ &= \log \frac{x^4(x-1)^2}{(x^2+1)^{1/3}} \end{align*}\]
